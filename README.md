## About me

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Computing Science at the University of Alberta, supervised by Prof. Grzegorz Kondrak and affiliated with Amii.

I am currently on the academic and industry job market, with expected graduation before May 2027. I am open to research scientist, applied scientist, NLP/LLM, and machine learning roles.

My research focuses on natural language processing, large language models, paraphrasing, lexical substitution, multilingual NLP, and language understanding.

## Research interests

- Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models
- Paraphrase generation, paraphrase identification, and lexical substitution
- Multilingual and cross-lingual NLP
- Text editing, controllable generation, and language understanding
