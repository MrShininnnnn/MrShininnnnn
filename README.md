## About me

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Computing Science at the University of Alberta, supervised by Prof. Grzegorz Kondrak and affiliated with the Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii). Prior to my Ph.D., I worked as a Senior Algorithm Engineer at Alibaba Group. I hold master’s degrees from the Georgia Institute of Technology, Syracuse University, and New York University.

I am currently on the academic and industry job market and am open to collaboration and career opportunities. Please feel free to get in touch!
